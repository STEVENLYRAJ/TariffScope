# TariffScope – Global Tariff Impact & Economic Shock Simulator

TariffScope is a data-driven economic intelligence platform that analyzes how global tariffs and geopolitical disruptions affect international trade, sector performance, and economic stability.

The platform provides interactive simulations that help users understand how policy decisions—such as a universal tariff increase or supply shocks in energy markets—can ripple through global supply chains and national economies.

Instead of presenting political opinions, TariffScope focuses on **neutral, data-based economic modeling** to help students, researchers, and policymakers explore trade policy impacts through interactive dashboards and scenario simulations.

---

## Key Features

### Tariff Impact Analyzer

Analyze how tariffs affect imports, trade volumes, and industry exposure across major economies and sectors.

### Search by Country or Product 

Explore tariff data by selecting specific countries or industries - View trade exposure and tariff-related insights instantly
### Universal Tariff Simulation

Simulate the economic impact of a **10% universal tariff scenario**, including estimated trade cost increases and sector vulnerability.

### Tariff Risk Score (0–100%) - 

Quantifies how vulnerable a country or sector is to tariffs - Based on trade dependency, sector exposure, and external risks

### Historical Tariff Trends -   

Visualize past tariff changes using interactive charts - Identify patterns and policy shifts over time

### Conflict → Economic Ripple Analysis

Visualize how geopolitical disruptions—particularly energy supply shocks—affect oil prices, inflation pressure, and economic exposure.

### Tariff Alerts System - 

Highlights industries likely to face tariffs - Early warning system based on trade patterns and global signals

## Vision

## Description

TariffScope is a data-driven economic intelligence platform that helps users understand how global tariff policies and geopolitical disruptions impact international trade, industry performance, and economic stability.

In today’s world, tariff decisions—such as proposed universal tariffs or sector-specific trade restrictions—can significantly influence product prices, supply chains, and global market dynamics. However, this information is often fragmented across multiple sources and difficult to interpret.

TariffScope solves this problem by providing a **unified, interactive platform** that transforms complex trade and policy data into clear, visual, and actionable insights.

The system enables users to:

* Analyze tariff exposure across countries and industries
* Track historical tariff trends and trade shifts
* Simulate real-world policy scenarios (e.g., a 10% universal tariff)
* Measure economic vulnerability using a custom **Global Exposure Index (GEI)**
* Understand how geopolitical events (such as energy supply shocks) influence trade dynamics

Additionally, TariffScope integrates an AI-powered news analysis module that identifies emerging trade tensions and dynamically updates tariff risk levels based on real-world signals.

Rather than presenting political viewpoints, the platform maintains a strictly **neutral and analytical approach**, focusing on economic modeling, data interpretation, and scenario-based forecasting.

TariffScope is designed for students, researchers, and decision-makers who want to explore how policy decisions and global events reshape the international economic landscape.

---

### Core Objective

To simplify complex global trade dynamics into an intuitive system that enables users to **analyze, simulate, and understand tariff-driven economic changes in real time.**
