# TariffScope – Global Tariff Impact & Economic Shock Simulator

TariffScope is a data-driven economic intelligence platform that analyzes how global tariffs and geopolitical disruptions affect international trade, sector performance, and economic stability.

The platform provides interactive simulations that help users understand how policy decisions—such as a universal tariff increase or supply shocks in energy markets—can ripple through global supply chains and national economies.

Instead of presenting political opinions, TariffScope focuses on **neutral, data-based economic modeling** to help students, researchers, and policymakers explore trade policy impacts through interactive dashboards and scenario simulations.

---

## Key Features

### Tariff Impact Analyzer

Analyze how tariffs affect imports, trade volumes, and industry exposure across major economies and sectors.

### Universal Tariff Simulation

Simulate the economic impact of a **10% universal tariff scenario**, including estimated trade cost increases and sector vulnerability.

### Conflict → Economic Ripple Analysis

Visualize how geopolitical disruptions—particularly energy supply shocks—affect oil prices, inflation pressure, and economic exposure.

### Global Exposure Index (GEI)

A custom metric designed to estimate a country's vulnerability to tariff and energy shocks based on trade dependency and energy imports.
