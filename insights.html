Pharma is the Critical Flashpoint
India's $8.7B pharmaceutical exports to the US face direct margin destruction. Generic drugs running on 12–18% margins cannot absorb a 26% duty without becoming unviable. Sun Pharma, Dr. Reddy's, Cipla and Lupin face a combined $3.2B annual revenue risk.

IT Services: The Structural Shield
India's $250B IT sector is exempt from goods tariffs by design. Services exports face zero direct tariff exposure. GCC additions hit a record 1,700+ in FY25. The China+1 digital shift is accelerating enterprise India-first strategies worth $35B+ over 5 years.

Relative Textile Advantage
India's 26% tariff is significantly lower than Vietnam (46%) and Bangladesh (37%), creating a surprise competitive opportunity. Tirupur manufacturers report 12–15% uptick in US buyer inquiries. Order diversion of $800M–$1.2B is feasible within 6–9 months.

China Decoupling Dividend
